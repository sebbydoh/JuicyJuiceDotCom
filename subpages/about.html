JUICYJUICE CULTURE

JuicyJuice strives to be a pillar in the community.


LOCALLY SOURCED

One of our biggest goals is to help the community grow by building a symbiotic relationship with the community. We believe that if our community grows, we grow too! We pride ourselves in offering beverages made from produce that are at least 80% locally sourced here in the Central Valley! We've partnered up with over 30 family-owned farms to help accomplish our goal of supporting the local economy.


FARMERS MARKET

JuicyJuice loves the Hanford Thursday Night Marketplace! Come see us give out free samples, as well as discount coupons for our drinks!


Family-owned

JuicyJuice has been family-owned for over 15 years! Serving families for over a decade is one of our greatest pride and joys!
